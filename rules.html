General Server Rules

1

You must be over 18 years old. If you enter by providing false information, all your access will be removed without any notice.

2

You are allowed to use only one character within the city. You may use clothing items that are appropriate to your character.They must match your character—for example, if you are a regular civilian in the city, you cannot wear police gear or uniforms.

3

A Situation Pause can only be requested if your team has 6 members, and at least two members have a valid issue (such as High PL, Disconnect, TimeOut, or other). A Situation Pause cannot be taken if only one member disconnects or experiences High PL or TimeOut. This rule applies to both teams.

4

Nothing that happens within the city will affect your real life, and just like in real life where you only have one life, the same applies within the city as well.

5

When creating your character, you must use a proper name. [Example: Titles like Mr/Mrs or symbols cannot be used as a name].

6

If you are riding a motorcycle, wearing a helmet and having a valid license is mandatory. If not, the police have full authority to stop and check you. In such a situation, you must continue the RP according to your character.

7

respect to the all players.

Roleplay Guidelines

1

New Life Rule (NLR)

If you are downed and respawn at the hospital, your character forgets all events leading up to you being downed in the current scenario When a new life starts, your character will forget all the information and events leading up to your death. However, you are still the same character (same job, gang, friends).​A new life starts:- when​ you are executed You re-spawn. A new life does not start when:​ Your character has been the victim of a clear case of VDM Your character has been the victim of a clear case of RDM You are downed (awaiting reviving).

2

No Vehicle Deathmatch (VDM).

Never use a vehicle to intentionally harm or kill anyone. VDM (Vehicle Deathmatch) means using a vehicle to injure or kill players on purpose or driving recklessly in a way that clearly causes death or injury. This includes running people over, causing vehicle explosions to kill others, or reckless high-speed driving that results in deaths.

3

No Random Deathmatch (RDM).

Do not kill players without any roleplay story leading up to it. You must always initiate RP and make others aware of your actions and reasons. However, in gang or group wars with proper storyline buildup, initiation may not always be required before shooting.

4

No combat logging.

Logging out during an active RP situation to avoid roleplay is strictly prohibited. This includes disconnecting during a firefight, police chase, arrest, while downed awaiting EMS, or while restrained/detained, as well as rage quitting or logging out to save gear. We understand crashes or connection issues may happen, but staff will review logs and expect you to rejoin and continue the situation properly.

5

No Breaking Character

Always stay in character (IC) at all times. If there is an issue, continue the roleplay scenario and discuss problems later out of character (OOC) after the situation ends. Do not break RP or use actions to bait others into breaking rules. This includes statements like “I will make a ticket,” “I will speak to an admin,” or “that is overpowered.” You must not approach staff in-game to resolve issues during active RP.

6

No Powergaming

Powergaming is using unrealistic RP or mechanics to gain unfair advantage, force outcomes, or limit others, actions. This includes forcing single-outcome RP, abusing /me, using radio/communication unrealistically, acting while downed, or preventing fair response/negotiation from other players.
