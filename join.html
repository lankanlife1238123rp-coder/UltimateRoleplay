HOW TO JOIN OUR SERVER

Follow these simple steps to join Ultimate Roleplay and start your adventure in Los Santos!

1

Join Our Discord

Our Discord server is the hub of our community. Join to get the latest updates, connect with other players, and access server information.

Join Discord
2

Read the Rules

Familiarize yourself with our server rules to ensure a positive experience for everyone. Breaking rules may result in warnings or bans.

Read Rules
3

Install Required Mods

You'll need to install FiveM and our required resource pack to join the server.

Download FiveM Resource Pack
4

Create Your Character

Once in the server, you'll need to create your character. Think about their backstory, personality, and goals to enhance your roleplay experience.

5

Connect to the Server

Open FiveM, click on "Play" and search for "Ultimate Roleplay" or use our direct connect address.

Server Name: Ultimate Roleplay
Direct Connect: connect play.Ultimate Roleplay.com
